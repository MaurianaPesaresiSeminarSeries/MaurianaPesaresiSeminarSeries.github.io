Mauriana Pesaresi

Seminar Series 2021/2022

The series of seminars dedicated to the memory of Mauriana Pesaresi is composed of a series of meetings organized and held by Ph.D. students of the Computer Science Department of the University of Pisa.

Seminars will be held weekly both remotely and in presence, starting from January 2022. Each seminar will be an opportunity for the students to present in public core issues of their research, focusing on open problems and possibly presenting some of their original results. Presentations should last approximately thirty minutes, leaving sufficient time to develop meaningful discussions.
